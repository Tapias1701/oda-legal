Last updated: 25 June 2026

Oda respects intellectual property rights and expects users to do the same. Users may not upload or share content they do not have permission to use.

Submitting a takedown notice

If you believe content in Oda infringes your copyright, email oda.app.support@gmail.com with a notice that includes:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature.
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
  3. Identification of the material claimed to be infringing and enough

information for us to locate it, such as username, post, comment, message, screenshot, date, or timestamp.

  1. Your contact information, including name, address, email, and phone number.
  2. A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized by

the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

  1. A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are

authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

Our process

When we receive a valid notice, we may remove or disable access to the reported material, notify the user who posted it, and record the action for repeat infringer review.

Counter-notices

If your content was removed and you believe the removal was a mistake, contact oda.app.support@gmail.com. Include your signature, the removed material and its location, your contact information, and a statement explaining why you believe the removal was mistaken or misidentified.

Repeat infringers

Accounts that are the subject of repeated valid copyright notices may be limited, suspended, or terminated.

DMCA designated agent

If Oda seeks U.S. DMCA safe-harbor protection, Konnectipus S.A.S. must maintain a current designated-agent registration with the U.S. Copyright Office and keep the public agent contact information current. This policy does not by itself guarantee safe-harbor protection.

Misuse

Submitting false or abusive notices may result in liability or account action. When in doubt, seek legal advice.
