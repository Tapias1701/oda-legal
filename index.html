Privacy Policy

Oda: Focus & Vision · Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Konnectipus S.A.S. (“we”, “us”, “Oda”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Oda: Focus & Vision mobile application (the “App”). By creating an account, continuing as a guest, or using the App, you authorize the processing described in this Policy.

1. Who we are

The data controller is Konnectipus S.A.S., based in Bucaramanga, Colombia. For any privacy question or request you can reach us at oda.app.support@gmail.com.

We collect only what the App needs to work. Depending on the features you use, this may include:

3. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and provide the App’s features.
  • To save your study progress, goals, streaks and trophies across devices.
  • To power optional features you enable: health insights, the AI study coach, the social feed, direct messages, and the study planner.
  • To process subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • To send notifications you have opted into (e.g., streak and study reminders).
  • To keep the community safe (e.g., handling reports and blocks) and to detect abuse.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your data for third-party advertising.

4. Health & wellbeing data (Apple Health / Google Health Connect)

Health data is optional and only accessed after you explicitly grant permission. Specifically:

  • Oda only reads health data; it never writes data back to Apple Health or Google Health Connect.
  • Health data is used solely to show you your own wellbeing insights inside the App (the “Apex” and “Metric” views) alongside your study performance.
  • Health data is stored under your private account and is never used for advertising or marketing, and is never sold or shared with third parties for their own purposes.
  • You can revoke health access at any time in your device’s Health / Health Connect settings, and you can delete the data we hold by deleting your account (Section 8).

5. AI study coach

The optional AI coach is powered by Google’s Gemini models. When you use it, the message you type and relevant study context are sent—through our secure server—to Google to generate a response. We apply rate limits and usage caps, and we restrict the assistant to study- and learning-related topics. Your prompts are processed to produce your answer and are not used to show you ads. You can report an AI response if it appears offensive, unsafe, or clearly inappropriate. Google processes this data as our sub-processor; see Google’s terms for the Gemini/Generative Language API.

7. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your personal data and content from our active systems, except where limited retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, dispute, backup, or moderation purposes. Content you shared with others may remain only where necessary to preserve another user’s own record or where law permits retention. Aggregated, anonymized statistics that cannot identify you may be retained.

8. Your rights and choices

  • Access & export: you can view and export your study data from within the App (Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Data).
  • Deletion: you can permanently delete your account and associated data at any time in the App (Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete Account). This is irreversible.
  • Correction: you can update account and profile details in the App where available, or contact us for help.
  • Revocation: you may revoke optional permissions in device settings and may request deletion or suppression of personal data as allowed by applicable law.
  • Blocking & reporting: you can block other users and report content or AI responses directly in the App.
  • Permissions: you can revoke camera, photos, health, calendar, and notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as Colombia’s Ley 1581 de 2012, the EU/UK GDPR, or the California CCPA/CPRA (including access, correction, deletion, revocation, and the right to object). To exercise any right, contact us at oda.app.support@gmail.com.

9. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures: encrypted connections (TLS), encryption at rest, row-level security so each account can only access its own data, and server-side validation of sensitive operations. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

10. Children’s privacy

Oda is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us data, contact us and we will take appropriate deletion or account action.

11. International data transfers

We operate from Colombia and use service providers that may store and process data in the United States and other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers. By using the App you consent to this processing.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide notice within the App. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
