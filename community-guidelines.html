Oda — Community Guidelines

Last updated: 25 June 2026
Contact: oda.app.support@gmail.com

Oda is a calm study and focus community. These rules apply to profiles, posts, photos, comments, direct messages, squads, and any other user-generated content inside Oda.

Not allowed

  • Harassment, bullying, threats, stalking, or abusive behavior.
  • Hate, discrimination, or dehumanizing content toward protected or vulnerable

groups.

  • Sexual content, nudity, graphic content, or anything sexualizing minors.
  • Violence, self-harm promotion, graphic injury, or encouragement of dangerous

behavior.

  • Impersonation, fake identities, scams, spam, malware, or platform abuse.
  • Posting private information about another person without permission.
  • Copyright, trademark, publicity, privacy, or other rights infringement.
  • Attempts to manipulate trophies, streaks, metrics, payments, AI quotas,

security systems, or moderation systems.

  • Illegal activity or instructions for illegal activity.

Expected behavior

  • Be respectful.
  • Share your own content or content you have permission to use.
  • Keep posts, squads, and messages aligned with studying, focus, and personal

growth.

  • Use reporting and blocking tools instead of escalating conflicts.

Reporting and blocking

You can report posts, comments, profiles, direct messages, and AI responses from inside the App. You can also block users you do not want to interact with.

Reports may be reviewed by Oda. We may remove content, hide content, limit features, suspend accounts, terminate accounts, or escalate serious violations.

AI content

AI responses in Oda are generated automatically and can be wrong or unsuitable. Do not use AI features to generate prohibited content. Report any AI response that appears offensive, unsafe, or clearly inappropriate.

Enforcement and appeals

Oda may enforce these Guidelines with or without notice when needed to protect users, comply with law, or protect the App. For questions or appeals, contact: oda.app.support@gmail.com.

Copyright issues are handled under the Copyright & Takedown Policy: https://tapias1701.github.io/oda-legal/copyright-dmca.html
