Oda includes optional AI-assisted study and focus features.

What Oda's AI does

Oda's AI coach helps with study planning, focus, productivity, reflection, learning, and motivation. It is designed for study-related guidance only.

AI can be wrong

AI responses are generated automatically. They may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, offensive, unsafe, or unsuitable for your situation. AI in Oda is not a human and is not professional, medical, psychological, legal, financial, or emergency advice.

Do not rely on AI responses for important decisions. If you need medical, mental-health, legal, financial, or emergency support, contact a qualified professional or local emergency services.

Provider

Oda's AI features are currently powered by Google Gemini through Oda's secure backend. Oda is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official product of Google.

What is sent to the AI provider

When you use AI features, your prompt and the app context needed to answer it may be sent to the AI provider to generate a response. This can include recent study context, goals, focus-session summaries, and any text you type into the AI coach.

Do not submit sensitive personal information, such as government identifiers, financial information, medical details, secrets, passwords, or private information about other people.

Your controls

AI features are optional. You can use Oda's focus, planning, progress, social, and privacy controls without asking the AI coach for guidance.

You can report an AI response in the App if it appears offensive, unsafe, or clearly inappropriate.
